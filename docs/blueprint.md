# **App Name**: InductFlow

## Core Features:

- User Authentication & Roles: Secure employee login/signup with email and password, supporting distinct access levels for Employees and Administrators.
- Employee Induction Dashboard: Personalized dashboard displaying welcome messages, a visual induction progress bar, and a list of assigned policies with their current completion status.
- Admin Policy Management & AI Enhancement: Administrators can upload and define new policies (PDF, video, text) with metadata. Includes an AI tool to automatically summarize policy content and suggest multiple-choice quiz questions based on the policy text.
- Policy Engagement & Compliance Tracking: Enables employees to view policies, tracking read time and scroll depth to ensure content engagement. Policies are marked complete only upon meeting specified engagement criteria, followed by a digital acknowledgement.
- Policy Quizzing Module: An optional module where policies can have associated quizzes (3-5 MCQs). Requires a passing score for policy completion, reinforcing understanding.
- Admin Reporting & Oversight: A comprehensive administrator dashboard to monitor employee-wise completion statuses, policy-wise compliance rates, and identify pending or non-compliant employees.
- Automated Notification System: Sends timely reminders to employees for pending policies and displays important alerts directly on their dashboard.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A sophisticated and trustworthy deep blue, establishing a professional and stable foundation (#4460A3).
- Background color: A very light, calming blue-grey for a clean and uncluttered interface, enhancing readability (#F2F5F8).
- Accent color: A vibrant yet harmonious cyan-blue, used to draw attention to interactive elements and progress indicators, signifying progress and clarity (#50C0D8).
- Body and headline font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for its modern, clean, and highly legible characteristics suitable for all text elements in a professional setting.
- Utilize modern, line-art style icons that are clear, intuitive, and consistent across the application to enhance navigation and comprehension.
- Employ a responsive dashboard layout featuring a persistent left-hand sidebar navigation. Content areas will use a card-based system to organize information, along with clear progress bars and status badges for visual cues.
- Subtle, functional animations for transitions, feedback upon action completion, and progress bar updates, aiming for a smooth and responsive user experience without distraction.
